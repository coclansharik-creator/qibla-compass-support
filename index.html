Common Questions

  • The compass seems inaccurate Try moving your phone away from metal objects and rotate your phone in a figure-8 motion to calibrate the compass.
  • The app asks for location access Location is required only to calculate the Qibla direction relative to your position. Your location is processed locally on your device and is not stored.
  • Purchases The app includes optional one-time tips to support development. These are processed securely through Apple.
